Services & Programs

Click the tabs below to explore the various services and programs we offer.

Please note: an application is required for some services. For your convenience, and to expedite the application process, we've provided some of these documents here for you to fill out before visiting our office.

Emergency Assistance Programs include:

  • Marketplace - food, toiletries, cleaning products, clothing and household items.
  • Financial Assistance - pay utility bills, rent and mortgage, birth certificates, etc.
  • The Ohio Benefit Bank - access public benefits (SNAP (food stamps), WIC, Medicaid, child care subsidies, federal & state income taxes, etc.)
  • Referrals to other organizations for services that MEAC does not provide.

Self-Sufficiency Programs include:

  • Early Childhood Literacy Program to help children (preschool through 3rd grade) who have reading deficiencies increase their reading abilities and confidence.
  • Adult Basic Reading Program through our partnership with the Literacy Network of Greater Cincinnati to teach literacy skills to adults who read at or below a 4th grade reading level.
  • Enhance Your Life – case management to provide support, information, resources, and other services to help prevent homelessness and increase income.
  • Cincinnati Works
  • Pro Bono Counseling

Seasonal Programs include:

  • Thanksgiving Baskets - food boxes and turkeys given out the Sunday before Thanksgiving.
  • Happy Holiday Shop - holiday shopping experience for clients who wish to have gifts for their household members around the Christmas season.
  • Swim Program - Cincinnati Recreation Center passes and swim apparel provided to household members.
  • School Back Packs - back packs with school supplies provided for students preschool through 12th grade.

Some of our other events include:

  • Monday Night Community Meal served weekly, excluding certain holidays (check our Event Calendar), and provided by host groups/churches open to the community. Bingo and raffle prizes are often provided.
