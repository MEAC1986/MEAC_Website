Financial Contributions

MEAC graciously accepts monetary donations to help support the work of the organization. You may send a check made payable to MEAC to 4600 Erie Avenue, Cincinnati, OH 45227. You may also make an online credit card donation by clicking on the Donate Now button below.









Volunteer

  • Answer telephones during business hours
  • Work in the MEAC Marketplace
  • Provide food and help serve a meal on Monday nights
  • Provide fun time (arts-n-crafts, storytime, etc.) for children during our community meal
  • Volunteer during special client and fundraising events throughout the year
  • Run errands on an as-needed basis
