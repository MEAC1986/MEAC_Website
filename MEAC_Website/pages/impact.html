WHAT'S HAPPENING


MEAC’s 2016 Statistics

  • Marketplace Food: Served 3,437 duplicated households; 7,192 duplicated people
  • Clothing: Served 3,120 duplicated households; 6,710 duplicated people
  • Thanksgiving: Provided dinners for 323 households; 717 people (35% were children)
  • Community Dinners: Served 2772 people served (1852 adults, 920 children)
  • Happy Holiday Shop: Gifts for 213 Families (330 adults and 235 children)
  • Financial Assistance: Served 187 duplicated households
  • Ohio Benefit Bank: Served 73 Adults
  • Prevent Blindness Ohio: 9 Adults received eye exams and eye glasses; 4 adults received eye glasses
  • Early Literacy Program: 59 Children enrolled in n2015/2016 school year; 26 children attended the MEAC Summer Reading Camp
  • Family Night Literacy Program: 342 people attended throughout the school year (230 children and 112 adults)
