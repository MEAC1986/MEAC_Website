OUR IMPACT


MEAC Marketplace

Julia stops at the MEAC marketplace on the first Tuesday of every month. She's able to pick up needed clothes and dry goods for her family. With the help of MEAC marketplace, Julia has been able to spend on a nicer house in a better neighborhood.


The MEAC Marketplace is open every Tuesday and Thursday from 9:30 am to 3:00 pm.

Monday Night Community Dinners

Helen is one of MEAC's Monday Night Community dinners regulars. Being worried about where she was going to eat next made Helen depressed and uninvolved with the people around her. Now, she has been able to not only meet people in similar situations, but also create a support group for her to socialize with and rely on.


Community Dinners are served from 5 - 6:30 pm on Monday nights.
